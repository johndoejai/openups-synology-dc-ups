# Bill of Materials (reference)

This is a starting point. Adapt to your load and local availability.

## Core
- Mini-Box OpenUPS (USB monitoring via NUT)
- 12.8 V LiFePO₄ battery pack with integrated BMS (e.g., 8 Ah class)
- 12 V PSU upstream (Synology 100 W adapter used in the reference setup)

## Distribution
- DC fuse block or inline fuses (**one per branch**)
- DC wiring + connectors (choose one standard, label polarity)
- Cable ties / strain relief

## Optional (high value for debugging)
- AC plug meter (for idle power)
- DC inline meter/shunt (to measure battery current in the mA range)
- 3D-printed enclosure (PLA) + M3 inserts/screws
